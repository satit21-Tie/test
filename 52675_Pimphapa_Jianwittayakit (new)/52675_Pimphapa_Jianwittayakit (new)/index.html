Fish – Eel – Stingray

An AI image classifier that recognizes three aquatic categories.

What this project is

This is a lightweight, browser‑based AI that classifies an uploaded image into one of three classes: Fish, Eel, or Stingray. It runs entirely on your device using TensorFlow.js and a model trained with Google Teachable Machine.

  • AI Type Used: Image Classification
  • Classes Trained: Fish, Eel, Stingray
  • Privacy Friendly: images never leave your browser
Upload a photo & classify →

How it works (at a glance)

  1. We resize your image to 224×224 pixels.
  2. The neural network extracts visual features.
  3. Softmax picks the most probable class.
